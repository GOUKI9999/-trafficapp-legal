Privacy Policy

Application: Red Green Light · Last updated: 2026-04-23

1. What data we collect

None. Red Green Light is a fully offline application. It does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information, device identifiers, location data, or usage analytics.

2. How data is stored

The only data the app stores is the traffic-light change records you enter yourself (timestamps and a direction flag R→G or G→R). These are kept exclusively on your device in the app's local database. Nothing leaves your device.

3. Network access

The app does not make any network requests. It functions without an Internet connection at all times.

4. Permissions

The app does not request any runtime permissions. It requires no location, camera, microphone, storage, contacts, or Internet access.

5. Children's privacy

This app does not collect information from any user. It is therefore suitable for users of all ages.

6. Third-party services

The app does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics providers, ad networks, or cloud services.

7. Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, a new version will be published at this same URL with the "Last updated" date revised.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy: gouki9999@gmail.com

隐私政策

应用：红绿灯预测 · 最后更新：2026-04-23

1. 我们收集什么数据

什么都不收集。红绿灯预测是一个完全离线的应用， 不收集、不传输、不共享任何个人信息、设备标识、位置数据或使用分析。

2. 数据如何存储

应用仅存储你手动输入的红绿灯变化记录（时间戳 + 方向标记 R→G 或 G→R）。 这些数据只保存在你设备上的应用本地数据库中。任何信息都不会离开你的设备。

3. 网络访问

应用不发起任何网络请求，在没有互联网连接时可完整使用。

4. 权限

应用不申请任何运行时权限。不需要位置、相机、麦克风、存储、通讯录或互联网访问。

5. 儿童隐私

本应用不收集任何用户信息，因此适合各年龄段用户使用。

6. 第三方服务

应用不接入任何第三方服务、分析工具、广告网络或云服务。

7. 政策变更

若本政策有更新，新版本会发布在同一 URL，"最后更新"日期会相应修改。
